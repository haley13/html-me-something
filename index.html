HOME

by Haley E. Allick

INT. HIBISCUS HOTEL-FLOOR 5-NIGHT

DAMIAN (28) admires a CONCH SHELL SCULPTURE in the elevator lobby. A HIBISCUS HOTEL, ST. CROIX plaque hangs on the CORAL STONE wall. Handsome, muscular, relaxed, he holds a CENTERLINE BAKERY bag. The SHINY GOLD elevator doors open. LOUD tourists flood the lobby. Damian looks at them exit and then enters the elevator. SASHA (28) runs up to the elevator in her blue SHIMMERY evening gown and silver SPARKLY high heels. Her DIAMOND hoop earrings GLISTEN in the light. She bumps into a TIPSY TOURIST who drops her book.

TIPSY TOURIST

Hey, watch here you’re going!

Sasha glances at the woman.

SASHA

Sorry.

The Tourist smiles and picks up her book.

TIPSY TOURIST

That’s okay, hon. Happy New Year!

Sasha rushes forward.

SASHA

Hold the elevator!

Damian reaches out his arm to stop the elevator. Sasha enters and the doors close.

INT. ELEVATOR-NIGHT

Sasha pushes the DOOR CLOSE button. They are both originally from the island and it’s clear she’s lost her CRUCIAN accent since she moved away.

DAMIAN

Going up?

SASHA

Sasha pushes the L for Lobby.

The elevator GRINDS TO A HALT. Sasha looks panicked. She pushes the EMERGENCY button multiple times. She SUCKS HER TEETH.

SASHA (CONT'D)

You have to be kiddin’ me!

Damian looks at her calmly. Sasha continues to push the EMERGENCY BUTTON.

DAMIAN

If you push it enough times, I’m sure it will work faster.

Sasha BANGS on the elevator doors.

SASHA

911 EMERGENCY.

Damian continues to stand calmly. Begins to hum. Sasha grimaces.

SASHA (CONT'D)

Can you stop?!

Damian stops humming. Sasha notices the CENTERLINE BAKERY bag. She folds her arms, relaxes a little.

SASHA (CONT'D)

Their Guava Tarts and Coconut Drops are my favorite.

Damian is surprised.

DAMIAN

Oh, yeah? What you know ‘bout Centerline?

Sasha laughs.

SASHA

I ate there for breakfast.

DAMIAN

How’d we do?

Sasha raises her eyebrows.

SASHA

Delicious, as always.

Damian smiles. Goes to shake her hand.

DAMIAN

I’m Damian.

They shake hands, there's a glint of interest in Sasha's eyes.

SASHA

I’m Sasha. Morgan Stanley, New York. I haven’t been home in 7 years.

DAMIAN

How you like livin’ in Babylon?

SASHA

I love it.

DAMIAN

Tinkin’ of movin’ back?

SASHA

Too much poverty and dysfunction.

Damian looks at her quizzically.

DAMIAN

And Babylon is so much betta? Sasha looks at him with more intrigue.

SASHA

I see your point.

There is an uncomfortable silence. Sasha starts to speak with her CRUCIAN accent.

SASHA (CONT'D)

I from Harrigan.

DAMIAN

Harrigan? No sah.

SASHA

Yes sah.

DAMIAN

I woulda neva guess that.

SASHA

Yeah man.

DAMIAN

Thas the roughest projects on St. Croix.

SASHA

I survived.

DAMIAN

I see that. You’s a smart and focused woman.

Sasha looks at him.

SASHA

Tanks.

She leans against the elevator door and looks up at the ceiling. Sighs.

SASHA (CONT'D)

I forgot how beautiful it does be down here.

The elevator motor begins to WHIRR. Sasha jumps up. It moves up and then stops again. Damian’s cell phone rings. He answers it.

DAMIAN

Yeah, I deh in ya. Okay, tanks.

He hangs up the phone.

DAMIAN (CONT'D)

Sasha, de building manager just give me de code. I now the responda on call.

Damian types in a code and the elevator doors open. Sasha is surprised. She smiles and steps into the lobby.

INT. LOBBY-NIGHT Sasha walks out quickly and then slowly turns around. They both speak in a full CRUCIAN accent.

SASHA

Come wit me?

DAMIAN

I on call, rememba?

SASHA

Oh...right.

DAMIAN

Happy New Year, Ms. Sasha.

SASHA

You mean, Happy Old Year’s Night?

They share a smile. Damian, looking nervous, steps forward and holds the door open.

DAMIAN

I gon be at the cafe tomorrow mahnin’ at 9 if you wan to meet me.

Damian steps back into the elevator. Sasha smiles at him. The elevator doors close.

FADE OUT
